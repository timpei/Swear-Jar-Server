What is Swear Jar?

Swear Jar is an Android app that helps you swear less on a daily basis.

A Virtual Swear Jar

Our app is a virtual swear jar that allows you to keep track of how often you type swear words on your mobile device.

A Dollar per Swear

We record every time you swear to your friends, and place money into your virtual swear jar.

A Chance to Give Back

Once your swear jar is full, donate the money collected to your favourate charity. (Or get publicly shamed for your bad mouth :P)

Analytics

Swear Jar provides users detailed reports of their swearing patterns and habits.

Favorite Phrases

Discover your go-to swear words and see the common words you use them with.

Taunting Times

See the times you swear the most and keep track of your foul language history.

Foul Friends

Find out which of your friends you swear the most at, and who swears most at you!
