What is Swear Jar?

Swear Jar is an Android app that helps you swear less on a daily basis.

A Virtual Swear Jar

Our app acts as a virtual swear jar that allows you to keep track of how much you type swear words on your mobile device.

A Dollar for A Swear

We track the times you swear to your friends, and places money into your virtual swear jar.

A Chance to Give Back

Once your swear jar is full, donate the money collected to your favourate charity. (Or get publicly shamed for your bad mouth :P)
